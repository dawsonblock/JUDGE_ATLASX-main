# Legal Risk Boundaries

JUDGE_ATLASX alpha enforces strict legal-intelligence boundaries.

## Prohibited Behaviors

- autonomous accusation
- corruption/guilt/danger/ideology scoring
- predictive policing outputs
- publication without evidence + review gate

## Required Behaviors

- evidence authority first
- review gate before public visibility
- auditable mutation trail
- AI outputs treated as suggestions only

## Non-Authority Notice

This system is not a legal authority and does not provide autonomous adjudication.
