# AI BOUNDARY RULES

## Allowed AI Usage
- Summarization/extraction assistance.
- Candidate entity/event linking for reviewer confirmation.
- Drafting non-binding notes tied to evidence references.

## Prohibited AI Usage
- Autonomous legal decisions or verdict assignment.
- Predictive policing/risk scoring on people.
- Publication of allegations without reviewer approval.
- Claims that are not traceable to canonical source evidence.

## Required Safeguards
- Human review required before publish.
- Evidence links must accompany AI-assisted conclusions.
- AI outputs must be treated as suggestions, not authority.
